MOCA Spike 150
Running Forward With Our Stories
Museum of Chinese in America
215 Centre Street New York, NY 10013
855-955-6622
2019 marks the 150th anniversary of the completion of the Transcontinental Railroad. The “MOCA Spike 150 - Running Forward with Our Stories!” initiative will honor the thousands of Chinese workers responsible for this great milestone in American History as well as explore the Chinese diaspora throughout America.
